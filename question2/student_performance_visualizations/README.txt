Gender: The student's gender, whether it be male or female.
Race/Ethnicity: A classification indicating the student's ancestry in terms of race or ethnicity.
Parental education level: The maximum educational level (college, associate's, bachelor's, master's, etc.) that the student's parents have obtained.
lunch: The student's lunch plan (regular, free/reduced, etc.) may provide information about their socioeconomic background.
Course on exam preparation: This indicates if the student completed a course on test preparation or not.
Math score: The numerical value indicating an individual's proficiency in mathematics.
Reading score: A numerical representation of a reader's proficiency.
The writing score is given as a numerical value.
